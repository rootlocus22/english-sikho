Staring at a blank "Compose" window? You're not alone. 65% of professionals struggle with email anxiety.

Whether you're asking for a raise, applying for leave, or sending a project update, the right words matter. Here are 5 essential templates you can copy-paste right now.

## 1. The "Sick Leave" Email
**Subject:** Sick Leave Application - [Your Name]

Dear [Manager's Name],

I am writing to inform you that I am unwell (suffering from a fever/migraine) and will generally be unable to come to the office today, [Date].

I have handed over my urgent tasks to [Colleague's Name]. I will remain available on phone for any critical emergencies.

Thank you for your understanding.

Best regards,
[Your Name]

## 2. The "Request for Meeting" Email
**Subject:** Request for Meeting: [Topic]

Hi [Name],

I would like to request a brief meeting with you to discuss [Project X / The New Proposal]. 

Would you be available sometime this week? Tuesday morning or Thursday afternoon works best for me, but I am happy to work around your schedule.

Best,
[Your Name]

## 3. The "Follow Up" Email (Gentle Reminder)
**Subject:** Re: [Project Name] Update

Hi [Name],

I'm writing to follow up on my previous email regarding [Topic]. 

Did you get a chance to review the documents? Please let me know if you need any further information from my end to proceed.

Thanks,
[Your Name]

## 4. The "Sending a Report" Email
**Subject:** Monthly Performance Report - [Month, Year]

Dear Team,

Please find attached the performance report for [Month]. 

**Key Highlights:**
*   Revenue increased by 10%
*   User acquisition up by 5%

Let me know if you have any questions.

Best regards,
[Your Name]

## 5. The "Thank You" Email (After Interview)
**Subject:** Thank you - [Job Title] Interview - [Your Name]

Dear [Interviewer's Name],

Thank you for taking the time to speak with me today about the [Role Name] position.

I enjoyed learning more about [Company Name]'s vision for the future. I am even more enthusiastic about the opportunity to join the team and contribute to [Specific Project discussed].

I look forward to hearing from you.

Sincerely,
[Your Name]
